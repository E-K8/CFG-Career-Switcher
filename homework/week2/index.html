E5 Bakehouse

Order ONLINE for home delivery or same day collection of our breads, pastries, treats, stone milled flour, home baking equipment, organically grown groceries and much more.

Serving up espresso, lattes, cappuccinos and flat whites, with our lunches on offer during the week too – drop by or place an order to collect from the Millhouse from
7.30am – 4.30pm EVERY SINGLE DAY.

E5 Bakehouse is an east London artisan bakery, cafe and mill. We are passionate about sourdough and the lost traditions of our baking ancestors. Using organic, locally-sourced ingredients, we serve delicious daily-baked breads, lunches, homemade cakes and our own small batch roasted coffee.
